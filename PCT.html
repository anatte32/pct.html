The story behind this idea:

Every true hiker familiar with the famous sentence: "Hike your own hike", which means; Hike your own way without trying to compare yourself with others. When i hiked the PCT on 2018, thets was my guiding sentence. Every time i felt desperation i thought about this sentence and cheerd up. Doring my journey,a funny idea came up to my mind; Using this sentence in its humoristic way. When i came back to israel, i sherd my ideas with my Sister. And with the help of her talented friend "Yonaten Gal" who draw all the items, we started to make shirts and cards with this drowings depicting the sentence.
